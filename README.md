# About Me

I'm a **Robotics & Embedded Systems Engineer** passionate about building intelligent systems that seamlessly integrate **Artificial Intelligence**, **Embedded Computing**, **Computer Vision**, **Autonomous Navigation**, and **Wireless Communication**.

Currently pursuing a **B.Tech in Electronics & Communication Engineering** at **CUSAT** while simultaneously completing a **B.Sc. in Data Science & Applications** from **IIT Madras**, I enjoy developing engineering solutions that bridge hardware and software for real-world applications.

My work primarily focuses on designing **low-cost autonomous robotic systems**, **multi-sensor fusion algorithms**, **Software Defined Radio applications**, and **AI-powered perception systems** capable of operating in challenging environments.

I believe the future belongs to engineers who can combine **software engineering principles** with **embedded intelligence**, enabling robots and autonomous platforms to perceive, reason, communicate, and act intelligently.
